# Honey Bear Brownies

## Ingredients

* 2 oz butter
* 3/4 cup sugar
* 1/2 cup honey
* 2 tsp. vanilla
* 2 eggs
* 1/2 cup plain flour
* 1/3 cup cocoa
* 1/2 tsp salt

## Instructions

1. Cream Together
   * 2 oz Â butter
   * 3/4 cup Sugar
  _Note: Cream means melt the butter slightly then mix till the colour is an off yellow_
1. Blend in
   * 1/2 cup honey
   * 2 tsp. vanilla
1. Beat in one at a time - 2 eggs
1. Combine together:
   * 1/2 cup plain flour
   * 1/3 cup cocoa
   *  1/2 tsp Salt
1. Gradually put flour mixture into egg sugar mixture
1. Pour into pan
1. Bake at 350 F or 180c for 25 - 30 min.
