# Scones

## Ingredients

* 8 oz Flour
* 1 oz Sugar
* 1 tbl Baking Powder
* 2 oz Margarine
* Pinch of Salt
* 2 Eggs
* 1/4 Cup Milk
* Raisins

## Method

1. Beat eggs first
2. Set oven for 200 degrees Celsius and cook for 12 minutes

## Alterations

For Whole Wheat version
